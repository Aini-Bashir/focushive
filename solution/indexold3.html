Once you join, you will be placed into appropriate teams based on your ability to follow directions.

Once you have applied, you will receive an invitation to our Slack Discussion Group. Join here and meet other members around the world.

We like self starters who explore before asking questions. After applying, see how much of our cloud training you can complete to determine what team you'll be on.
